NOW STREAMING: RESEARCH PROJECT

Netflix and the Future of Entertainment

Streaming platforms are becoming the dominant way individuals consume entertainment. This project studies how Netflix can operate as both groundbreaking innovation and a structural threat.

Through case studies, media scholarship, and data visualizations, this site examines platform power, recommendation systems, global expansion, and shifting access to cultural visibility.

Start with Innovation vs. Threat Open Case Studies

Featured Question

To what extent does Netflix function as an innovative force in global entertainment, and to what extent does its dominance pose a cultural or structural threat to traditional media industries and the future of entertainment?
